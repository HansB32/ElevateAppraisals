Residential Real Estate Appraisals Clear valuation for homeowners, estates, and lending partners.

Why clients request this

  • Market positioning for listing, purchase, or FSBO negotiations
  • Estate, trust, and tax planning with reliable date-of-death values
  • Family law and partnership settlements needing impartial support
  • Refinance, HELOC, or PMI removal based on current equity

Typical assignments & deliverables

  • Single-family homes, custom builds, and high-end residences
  • Condominiums, townhomes, and PUD units
  • 2–4 unit income properties with rent schedule analysis
  • Unique rural cabins and recreational retreats
  • PDF narrative report with comparable grid, photo log, and market exhibits
Land & Site Appraisals Confident land valuation for acquisition, development, and estate work.

Why clients request this

  • Site acquisition or pre-construction budgeting
  • Subdivision, lot release, and feasibility studies
  • Estate gifting, charitable contributions, or tax planning
  • Agricultural, recreational, or transitional land decisions

Investment & Income-Producing Properties Decision-ready analysis for small residential portfolios and hybrid owners.

Why clients request this

  • Underwriting for DSCR, portfolio, or private placement loans
  • Hold/sell decisions and quarterly portfolio check-ins
  • Rental market studies to support lease renewals or repositioning
  • Partner buy-in/out, recapitalization, or succession planning

Typical assignments & deliverables

  • Form 1004 or 1025 market value opinions for 1–4 unit rentals
  • Form 1073 condominium investor reports
  • Form 1007 rent schedules with market-supported adjustments
  • Operating statement review with income capitalization
  • Rent roll cross-check, sensitivity tables, and PDF appraisal report

Note on small multifamily: For duplexes through fourplexes we commonly use Fannie Mae's Form 1025 (Small Residential Income Property Appraisal) when unit-level income data is required and a full market value opinion is requested. Form 1025 includes rent and income reconciliation alongside the value conclusion.
